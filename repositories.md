### Additional Similar Public Datasets

- **Aurora Multi-Sensor Dataset (Uber/Aurora):** [https://registry.opendata.aws/aurora_msds/](https://registry.opendata.aws/aurora_msds/)  
  Multi-sensor driving / localization data across diverse conditions.

- **Marine Energy Data Lake (DOE):** [https://registry.opendata.aws/marine-energy-data/](https://registry.opendata.aws/marine-energy-data/)  
  Marine and hydrokinetic energy datasets hosted on AWS.

- **End-Use Load Profiles (U.S. Building Stock):** [https://data.openei.org/submissions/4520](https://data.openei.org/submissions/4520)  
  High-resolution electricity usage by end use and building type.

- **openDD Roundabout Trajectory Dataset:** [http://www.l3pilot.eu/openDD](http://www.l3pilot.eu/openDD)  
  84,000+ vehicle trajectories at roundabouts, HD maps, and drone imagery.  
  Related paper: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.08463](https://arxiv.org/abs/2007.08463)

- **Zenseact Open Dataset (ZOD):** [https://zod.zenseact.com](https://zod.zenseact.com)  
  Large-scale multimodal driving dataset (LiDAR, cameras, radar).  
  Paper: [https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.02008](https://arxiv.org/abs/2305.02008)

- **Vehicle Energy Dataset (VED):** [https://github.com/gsoh/VED](https://github.com/gsoh/VED)  
  GPS + fuel/energy/time-series data from ~383 personal vehicles.  
  Paper: [https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.02081](https://arxiv.org/abs/1905.02081)

- **comma2k19 (comma.ai):** [https://comma.ai/comma2k19](https://comma.ai/comma2k19)  
  ~33 hours of highway driving data with video, GPS, IMU, and CAN bus.  
  Paper: [https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.05752](https://arxiv.org/abs/1812.05752)
